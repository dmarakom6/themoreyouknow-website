Life After Covid-19

By Melissa Adamantidi

Introduction

Since the covid-19 pandemic began, our lives have undergone major changes and now our new reality differs dramatically from the past one. Will our lives ever be the same or will we have to adjust to the new normal and never look back? We are all dying of curiosity to learn the answer to this question, however only time will tell. In the meantime, an analysis has been made on how covid has affected the fields of education, entertainment, technology, working conditions, as well as shopping.

Education

When covid-19 struck and it was no longer safe to attend school in person a new means of educating people had to be found. This is how we came up with online classes and even though they are obviously not the ideal way of acquiring knowledge, we can at least continue being taught despite the difficult circumstances. Most of us believe that e-learning is a temporary situation, however is this the case? Since this new school method appeared, a new era on education began. Children who live on remote locations, where there are no nearby schools, now have access to education provided that they have an electronic device. In addition, university/college students, who wish to attend foreign institutions but do not have the money required to start a new life there, can do their studies from home. Furthermore, whenever there are dangerous weather conditions or when it is the season of diseases, students can still get on with their material, while being safe under their own roof. Moreover, a new international school system will possibly be developed, which could be attended by students from all around the world, who will be united by technology.

Deliveries

During the period of quarantine, e-shopping rose nearly 60% and as a result there was a sudden need for new ways of performing quick deliveries and to serve this purpose various start-ups were created. A case in point is drone deliveries. Especially in the case of restaurants and cafes, where the distance from the store to the house is usually short, drones avoid traffic or any other distraction and bring the goods quickly and efficiently.

Autonomous in-store shopping

Major US retailers and e-commerce giants, such as Amazon have prototyped once again and created stores, where consumers can enter, select their products, put them in their basket, check out, pay via their smartphone and walk out without having had any human interaction or waited in long ques. This is the future of in-store shopping.

Biotechnology

A field which has played a core role during this pandemic, is biotechnology. Biotechnology is a combination of medicine-biology, technology and the basics of engineering. Some of the most significant achievements of this domain are the following: 1) gene editing, 2)3D bioprinting, 4)creation of artificial organs, 5)biosensors. Biotech developed really quickly this period, making it possible to have vaccines with over 90% efficacy in the span of 10 months instead of 10 years.
